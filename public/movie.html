The Best Movies According to Aditya

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Zodiac

It's one of the best movies of Jake Gyllenhaal. I love the overall running sequence of the movie. The gripping way it develops and its set environment.

Everyday

I love how this movie is so unrealistic yet it's able to give a real world feel of why you need love and how it changes your life. It has a special place in my list.

Ghajini

It has a great story and actors have performed perfectly.
